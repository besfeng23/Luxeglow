# **App Name**: LuxeGlow Premier

## Core Features:

- Premium Editorial Homepage: A visually striking homepage featuring a luxury hero, highlighted treatments, packages preview, and clinic experience, establishing immediate brand prestige and welcoming clients.
- Detailed Services & Packages Showcase: Elegant and organized presentation of all available treatments, signature services, and promotional packages, complete with refined pricing modules and in-depth descriptions.
- Refined Visual Gallery & Experience Section: A high-resolution photo gallery showcasing premium treatment results, the opulent clinic ambiance, and the overall luxurious client experience through editorial-style image placement.
- Online Contact & Inquiry Form: A secure and aesthetically integrated form for prospective clients to submit inquiries, request appointments, or connect with the lounge, featuring location details and Google Maps integration.
- AI Treatment Recommender Tool: An interactive tool that leverages AI to suggest suitable Luxe Glow treatments or packages to users based on their expressed beauty concerns, offering a personalized client journey.
- Mobile-First Responsive Design: Ensuring a consistent, high-end user experience and elegant layout adaptation across all devices, maintaining premium aesthetic and functionality from desktop to mobile.
- SEO-Optimized Site Structure: An underlying technical architecture designed for optimal search engine visibility, incorporating proper metadata, semantic HTML, and fast loading performance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme, evoking soft luxury and calm sophistication.
- Primary accent color: Muted Dusty Rose (#B99CA4), selected for its soft feminine elegance and luxurious appeal.
- Background color: Near-white Ivory Pearl (#F5EDED), providing a serene, spacious canvas with a subtle warm undertone.
- Secondary accent color: Restrained Warm Gold (#D6C29F), for understated opulence and key visual highlights, such as subtle borders or interactive elements.
- Text color: Deep Charcoal (#333333), offering strong readability and a sophisticated contrast against the light background, aligning with the clinic's refined credibility.
- Headlines and display text: 'Playfair' (serif), chosen for its modern elegance and high-fashion editorial aesthetic, reflecting the brand's premium identity.
- Body text and interface elements: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif), offering clear readability, modern luxury, and a warm, inviting feel that complements the sophisticated serif headlines.
- Minimalistic yet rich iconography; utilizing refined line art or subtle, single-color filled icons that harmonize with the brand's aesthetic, possibly in the warm gold accent color.
- A luxury editorial layout with refined compositions, ample elegant spacing, clear hierarchical structures, layered cards, and subtle shadows to create depth and visual richness.
- Subtle and sophisticated micro-animations for interactive elements and transitions, enhancing the premium user experience without distracting from content or creating visual clutter.
